Howdy, I'm Courtney.

I work on predictive modeling of biological sequences with applications in enzyme design, therapeutics, and variant effect prediction. I have a massive interest in determining if models are useful and/or building models that address real biological needs.

I am currently visiting at DeepMind with the Predictive Genomics Team. Previously I did my PhD with Harvard Systems Synthetic Biology under Debora Marks (2026). B.S. in Computer Science and Genetics from Clemson University (2019).

Outside of lab I bike, sew, run, spend time with my partner and our two beloved felines.
